# Far Trading Post

## Introduction // Summary
Far Trading Post (working title) is a game in which the player exchanges trade goods with NPC actors, making use of NPC trade good preferences and globally changing price trends to turn a profit.

## Game Feel and Genre
Far Trading Post is intended to offer a semi-educational, problem-solving challenge in the wrapper of a trading Simulation Game. It's intended to allow the player to progress at their own pace and make learning decisions, mistakes and triumphs.

## Ludic Principles

### Resource Scarcity

The player has several resources at their disposal, each of which exists in limited supply. Therefore managing these resources is key to success. At the base level these resources are Time, Currency, Trade Goods, but could further be expanded with resources such as Storage Capacity and Reputation.

As Time progresses the player's Currency is subject to attrition (upkeep costs), while the Supply and Demand of various Trade Goods will fluctuate. The primary intended means to succeed is to exploit these Price Trends to buy low and sell high.


## Theme and Style

Far Trading Post is designed to be setting-agnostic, but the version of the package that will be presented for viewing and testing will lean into a Sci-Fi aesthetic in both the interface and resources.

## Backstory // Premise

The player takes on the role of a broker in a semi-remote trading post at the edge of an interstellar empire. The space-station they reside in is frequently visited by traders that make the rounds between settled planets.
